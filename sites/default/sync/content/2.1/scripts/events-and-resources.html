ICRP Events

Content ICRP’s mission is to improve access to information about global cancer research funding and enable cancer research funding organizations to work collaboratively to address common challenges.

Our annual meetings are a great opportunity to connect with other cancer research funders, explore options for collaborative funding and exchange innovative ideas. Registration is free – please see below for information about past and forthcoming meetings.
